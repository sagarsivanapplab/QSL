Al Ahli Stadium

Al Ahli Stadium

qsl teams Al Ahli & Al Sailiya

Capacity 12000

The Hamad bin Khalifa stadium is the home of QSL side Al Ahli, the oldest football team in Qatar. The stadium was renovated in 2015 to boost it’s capacity to 18,00 seats. Along with QSL action, the stadium also regularly hosts athletics meets.
